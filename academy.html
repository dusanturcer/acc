Introduction to ACC Croquet Academy

In this section of the ACC website we will be providing all essential information with regards to the game of croquet. Starting from history, rules and variants, going through the basic strategies and tactics and finishing with advanced techniques of the game. We will give you advice on how to become a better player, how to practice and how to improve your shooting routine. We will also discuss common mistakes and faults and the ways how to avoid them.


You can find all available topics in the left panel area and we will be adding new ones hopefully on a biweekly basis. Enjoy the content and feel free to leave us your feedback at algarvecroquetclub@gmail.com
