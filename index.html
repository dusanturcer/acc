Portuguese Croquet Association

Who we are

Portuguese Croquet Association is a croquet organization governing the croquet life in Portugal.

Our ambitions are to:

  • Establish more venues across the Algarve
  • Encourage greater participation in Croquet
  • Arrange international events and interclub competitions
  • Establish membership of the World Croquet Federation
